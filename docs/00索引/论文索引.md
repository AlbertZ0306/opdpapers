## Priority Reading Order

1. **Rethinking On-Policy Distillation of Large Language Models: Phenomenology, Mechanism, and Recipe** (Tsinghua / OPD project) - Mechanistic failure-mode study of OPD; proposes cold start and teacher-aligned prompt recipes.
2. **Self-Distilled RLVR** (Microsoft Research / academic collaboration) - Diagnoses OPSD information leakage and proposes RLSD direction/magnitude decoupling.
3. **Learning beyond Teacher: Generalized On-Policy Distillation with Reward Extrapolation** (Tencent Hunyuan) - Generalizes OPD as dense KL-constrained RL and proposes ExOPD/G-OPD.
4. **Uni-OPD: Unifying On-Policy Distillation with a Dual-Perspective Recipe** (Tencent Hunyuan / multimodal group) - Unified OPD across LLMs and MLLMs with student exploration and teacher margin calibration.
5. **Lightning OPD: Efficient Post-Training for Large Reasoning Models with Offline On-Policy Distillation** (MIT Han Lab) - Offline OPD via teacher consistency and precomputed teacher log-probs over SFT rollouts.
6. **Nemotron-Cascade 2: Post-Training LLMs with Cascade RL and Multi-Domain On-Policy Distillation** (NVIDIA) - Industrial multi-domain OPD pipeline for consolidating domain-specialized RL teachers.
7. **Rubric-based On-policy Distillation** (NUS / OPD group) - ROPD replaces teacher logits with prompt-specific semantic rubrics for black-box OPD.
8. **Entropy-Aware On-Policy Distillation of Language Models** (KAIST / University of Toronto / Vector Institute / IBM Research) - Switches/augments reverse KL with forward KL when teacher entropy is high.
9. **TIP: Token Importance in On-Policy Distillation** (Princeton University / OPD group) - Token-level importance taxonomy for memory-efficient OPD.
10. **Rebellious Student: Reversing Teacher Signals for Reasoning Exploration with Self-Distilled RLVR** (Microsoft Research) - RLRT reinforces successful student tokens that deviate from privileged self-teacher predictions.
11. **Stable On-Policy Distillation through Adaptive Target Reformulation** (KAIST/Seoul National Univ. group) - Veto target reformulation for stabilizing OPD under large teacher-student gaps.
12. **On-Policy Context Distillation for Language Models** (Microsoft Research) - OPCD trains on student trajectories against a context-conditioned teacher.
